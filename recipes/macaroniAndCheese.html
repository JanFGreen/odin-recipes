Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Looking for a homemade alternative to boxed mac and cheese that's just as quick and easy? This stovetop mac and cheese uses staple ingredients and comes together in just 25 minutes.

You'll want to bookmark this recipe for busy weeknights. The good news is, it's so simple that once you make it a couple times, you'll likely have it memorized.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil elbow macaroni al dente
  2. Melting butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  3. Add flour
  4. Add salt and black paper and stir until smooth
  5. Slowly pour in milk and stir until the mixture is smooth and bubbling
  6. Add cheese! Slowly stir in Cheddar cheese until smooth and melted
  7. Add macaroni to into cheese sauce and mix
